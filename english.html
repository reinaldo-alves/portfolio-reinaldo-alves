Landing pages

A landing page is a web page designed to convert visitors into actions, such as sign-ups, purchases, or downloads. They stand out for their simplicity, with targeted content and a prominent call to action (CTA). Widely used in marketing campaigns.

An institutional website is a company's digital face, providing information about its history, values, services, and contact details. It's a platform that establishes credibility and helps the brand build trust and authority in the digital world, creating a connection with the customer.
